Specialized Survey Services

Turning Movement Counts

Assess vehicle patterns and movements across multi-leg intersections. These surveys enable traffic engineers to document the direction and classified volume of turning movements, normally split into 15 minute intervals.

Mid Block / Link Counts

Two way counts classified by vehicle type, and broken down in 15 minute segments. Essential for understanding traffic flow patterns on road segments.

Pedestrian Counts

Count the volume per direction of travel/crossing of pedestrians, generally at intersections or crossings. Includes public transport hubs. Data is normally segregated into 15 minutes.

Queue Length Surveys

Normally undertaken with turning movement counts, these surveys record the maximum number of vehicles queueing per lane in each 5 minute interval.

Cyclist Counts

Similar to pedestrian counts, these surveys count the volume per direction of travel/crossing of all cyclists, generally at intersections or crossing points, split into 15 minute intervals.

Journey Time Surveys

Use portable GPS units to quantify travel time and delay along corridors. Data includes maximum, minimum, and average speeds at defined route points.

Parking Surveys

Assess usage trends and occupancy rates across on-street or off-street areas. Provide arrival and departure times, duration of stays, and percentage capacity insights.

Illegal Movement Surveys

Identify and classify prohibited traffic movements at locations of client interest, backed up by video footage where needed for comprehensive documentation.

Public Transport Surveys

Provide passenger boarding and alighting counts for rail and bus services. Generally counted in volume and split into 15 minute intervals, tailored to transport network requirements.

Mini HD Video Surveillance

Our sophisticated in-house built mini HD CCTV cameras are technologically advanced, and allow us to undertake and provide in-depth data and analysis.

These cameras are capable of providing continuous real-time recording in full HD, and with over 500 of them to deploy on any given project, we are capable of undertaking the largest scale of survey, even at short notice.

Installation and placement

The camera units are fully self-contained and attached at the top of the lighting column. There are no pavement based units to cause trip-hazards in compact locations, or obvious units to potentially impact on typical junction performance.

Review and Analysis

The footage can then be provided on a hard drive to the client, to allow any further analysis or verification to be undertaken at any point in the future.
